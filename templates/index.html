How to Use
CCGT Material Control System — User Guide
1
Dashboard
Monitor project KPIs — BOM, Received, Issued, and Stock quantities. The ISO Readiness donut chart shows stage distribution. Click any segment to filter the ISO table below by that stage (Erection Ready / Spool Ready / Spool In Prog / Critical).
2
MatCode Master
Browse and verify all registered material codes with their categories, descriptions, and units of measure. Use this as a reference before entering BOM or receiving data.
3
BOM Management
View the full Bill of Materials organized by System and ISO Drawing. Filter by system, ISO, or category to quickly locate specific items and check required quantities.
4
Material Receiving
Log incoming materials by entering PKG NO, Mat Code, and received quantity. Each entry updates the stock ledger in real time and is reflected on the Dashboard KPIs.
5
Material Requisition (MR)
Issue materials to a specific ISO Drawing in three steps:
  1. Search for an ISO Drawing → select BOM items → click Add to MR to add them to the Pending MR Table.
  2. Review the MR Table — PKG NO is auto-allocated from available stock in ascending order.
  3. Click Confirm & Generate Material Issue Slip → preview the A4 slip → Confirm Print & Process Issue to print and record the transaction.
6
Material Stock
View current stock levels per Mat Code. Stock is calculated as: Received − Issued. Items with low stock or shortfalls are highlighted for easy identification.
7
MR History
Search and review all past material issue records. Filter by date range, ISO drawing number, or status to trace material movements and verify issued quantities.
